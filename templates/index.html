Hanging Cross

Hi, my name is Charles Johnson and I have fallen in love with creating meaningful pallet art. The picture above demonstrates the faith aspect that I have incorporated into my work. This journey started during the COVID-19 pandemic, in the basement of my house. Over that span of time, I have been able to interact and converse with those who share the same faith as I do. With that being said, I also have been able to sell over 60 of these unique crosses to people all over. One of my goals, is to make/sell these crosses while generating a profit. However, the most important thing to me is that I continue to spread the word of God. Below I have listed information about these crosses that can clear up any questions you may have. I am looking forward to meeting and speaking with you soon! Thank you for your time.
